Methodology

Three pipelines.
One decision.

DermaFusion mirrors how a dermatologist actually reasons — combining what they see with what they know about the patient.

01
👁
Visual Stream
MobileNetV2 extracts 1280-dimensional feature vectors from dermoscopic images after hair removal and colour normalisation preprocessing.
02
📋
Clinical Stream
A Multi-Layer Perceptron processes patient age, sex, and anatomical site into a 32-dimensional clinical feature vector — context a clinician would naturally consider.
03
🧠
Fusion + Grad-CAM
Both vectors are concatenated and passed through a classifier. Grad-CAM then backpropagates to highlight exactly which pixels drove the prediction.
